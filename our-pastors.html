Rev'd.Christopher
Okolo

Rev'd. Chris Okolo is a lover of Jesus called by God
to break people out of ignorance through the instruments of the Word and Spiritual Sounds

He is the lead pastor of THE SHARED LIFE ASSEMBLY, a fast growing church
in the city of Enugu, geared at raising young men and women who express the God life in every sphere of society.

He's also released a couple of songs which are blessing lives
namely -Ifunanya, Heart's desire, If You don't fix me, My Confidence
and recently Love like rain.

He is married to Rev'd.Favour Christopher and together they have a son-Magnificence Christopher -Okolo
